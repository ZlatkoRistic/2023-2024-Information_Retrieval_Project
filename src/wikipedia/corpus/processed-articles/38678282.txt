Stewie (c. 2005 – February 4, 2013) was the world's longest domestic cat, according to the Guinness Book of World Records.[2][3][4] Stewie was measured at 123 centimetres (48 in) on August 28, 2010.[2]
Stewie died on February 4, 2013, from cancer at his home in Reno, Nevada, at age 8.[5][6]
